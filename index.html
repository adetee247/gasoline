About us

Gasoline Associates International is one of the private companies licensed by the DPR To establish private petroleum refineries in Nigeria, situated in Ipokia, Ogun State precisely. It's the only petroleum refinery in Nigeria that is with a fully vertically integrated technology covering all aspects of refining, petrochemicals and gas. In joint partnership with FL Engineering Consortium, it has established a 100,000bpd Petroleum Refinery & Petrochemical Plant, expandable to a 250,000 BPSD capacity, for several reasons we will maintain the 100,000bpd.
